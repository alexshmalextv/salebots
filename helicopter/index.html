What you will learn

  • How to work with power, risk, and status in big deals without manipulation or cheap tricks.

About the author

The playbook is written by a practitioner who spent years in B2B sales and complex deals: from compressors and generators to real estate and investment assets. The material is based on real negotiations, failed and closed deals, not on abstract “sales theory”.

The goal of the book is simple: make you dangerous in big deals — in a good way. So that you understand what is really going on at the table, where the money is lost, and what exactly to do next.

FAQ about the book

Is this book for beginners in sales?
If you have never sold anything in your life, it may be heavy. The book assumes you already understand basic sales dynamics and want to move into bigger, more complex deals.
Does it only apply to helicopters and aircraft?
No. “Helicopter” here is a metaphor for any high-ticket purchase: real estate, yachts, industrial equipment, investment products, large consulting projects. The mechanics of risk, status, and decision are similar.
Is this about manipulation and hard closing?
No. The book is built around coordination of complex decisions, not tricks. You will not find “pressure closes” here. The goal is to make the right decision easier and safer for the buyer.
